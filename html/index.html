Dice Game

Rules and How to Play:

  1. There will be three rounds.
  2. Each round, you and the computer will both roll a pair of dice.
  3. Each round you will earn a score based on your dice roll:
    • Your score for that round is the sum of the two dice values
    • If you roll doubles (e.g. a 3 and a 3), then your score will be multiplied by 2.
    • Rolling a 1 will give you a score of 0 for that round.
  4. After three rounds, the player with the highest total score wins.
